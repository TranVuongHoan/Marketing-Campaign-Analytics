# 4. Report Creation

## Interactive Dashboard

The file `marketing_dashboard.html` is a fully self-contained interactive dashboard with 14 charts. Open in any web browser.

## Dashboard Sections

### Section 1 — Platform Mix
- ROAS by platform (bar)
- Revenue by platform (bar)
- CTR% vs Conv Rate% quality map (bubble)
- Funnel efficiency by platform (grouped bar)

### Section 2 — Brand Portfolio
- Revenue vs Spend scatter by brand
- ROAS by brand
- ROAS by industry vertical (bar)
- Revenue by vertical (bar)

### Section 3 — Timing & Seasonality
- Spend vs Revenue by month (dual axis)
- Monthly ROAS with golden windows highlighted
- ROAS by season (Q1-Tet, Q2-Spring, Q3-Summer, Q4-Sale)
- Campaign count and revenue by quarter

### Section 4 — Market & Agency
- Revenue by city
- ROAS efficiency map by city
- ROAS by agency
- CTR & Conv rate by competitor activity level
